Shrub Plants

Shrubs are woody Plants of relatively low height, having several steams arsing from the base and lacking a single trunk. They are also known as Buses, especially when they are much-branched and dense,while others are tall and thin. Some are leafy green, while others display coloueful flowers. They grow upward 10 feet(about 3 meters). Shrubs can be Flowering and Ornamental. Shrubs are an important feature of the landscape planting, being used for formal decorative groups, hedges, screens, and background plantings, to which they contibute pattern, colour, fragrancw, or utility. An area of cultivated shrubs in a park or garden is called Shrubbery.Some examples are: Hibiscus,Viburnum,Boxwoods, etc.
